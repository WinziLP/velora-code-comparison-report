Forensic Interpretation

A high percentage of equal lines indicates structural and functional overlap. Added lines on the right usually indicate wrappers, endpoint replacements, or branding adjustments around inherited logic.

Removed lines on the left indicate functionality no longer present in the right-side artifact. Hashes are shown side-by-side per file: matching hashes indicate byte-identical or line-normalized-identical content.

For fairness and precision, whitespace-only rows are excluded from changed-line metrics. In this report, a changed line always refers to meaningful code, not blank-line movement.
